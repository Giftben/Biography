Nnamdi Azikiwe

First President of Nigeria from 1963 to 1966

Here is a timeline of Dr Nnamdi Azikiwe

  • 1904  -Born in Zungeru, Niger state, Nigeria
  • 1930  -Received his BA degree in Political Science
  • 1933  -Appointed a full-time lecturer in Political Science
  • 1954  -Premier of Eastern Nigeria
  • 1960  -Sworn in as the first indigenous Governor-General and Commander-in Chief of the Federation of Nigeria
  • 1963  -He was made the First President of Nigeria.
  • 1966  -He was forced out of office by a deadly coup
  • 1978  -Founded the Nigerian Peoples Party (NPP)
  • 1996  -Died at the University of Nigeria Teaching Hospital.
  • 1996  -Buried at his country home in Onitsha.

"This page was built to remember the great Zik of Africa, who left us on May 11, 1996. He is gone, but he is not forgotten. His dreams, ideas, and beliefs still live on in many Nigerians including myself. We will dearly miss him. He was my best friend, my mentor, my role model, my motivator, and most importantly, my father. As long as I live, I would make sure that the people who he fought for their freedom remember him. I shall also make sure that the poor and powerless Nigerians have a voice."

"UWAKWE AZIKIWE"
